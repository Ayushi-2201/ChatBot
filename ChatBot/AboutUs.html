About Us

Our Story

We are a family-owned pizza outlet that has been serving delicious pizza since 2005. Our passion for pizza started when our founder, Delight, visited Italy and fell in love with the authentic Italian pizza. He brought back the recipe and started making pizza in his backyard for friends and family. The pizza was so good that he decided to open a pizza outlet and share his love for pizza with the world.

Our Pizza

We make our pizza with the freshest ingredients and the finest cheese. Our dough is made fresh every day and we use a secret blend of spices to give it that authentic Italian flavor. We have a wide variety of toppings to choose from, including pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, peppers, and more. We also have vegetarian and vegan options available.

Our Service

We are committed to providing our customers with the best service possible. We offer fast and friendly delivery and take pride in our customer service. We also have a dine-in option available for those who want to enjoy their pizza in a cozy atmosphere.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the best pizza experience possible. We are committed to using only the freshest ingredients and the finest cheese to make our pizza. We are also committed to providing excellent customer service and making our customers feel like family.
