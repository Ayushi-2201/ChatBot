How to Make Pizza Dough

Making pizza dough from scratch is easier than you might think. Here's our recipe:

  1. Combine 2 cups of flour, 1 tablespoon of sugar, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 1 tablespoon of yeast in a large bowl.
  2. Add 1 cup of warm water and 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the bowl and stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a towel, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a pizza shape.
  6. Add your favorite toppings and bake at 425°F for 12-15 minutes.
